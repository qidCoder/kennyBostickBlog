Trip to Attu, Alaska

Arrived at the Island and headed to my private room at the back of the bunker. I saw Mrs. Suzuki and gave her a high five and then sat back to have some cereal while the others chased around a bunting. That old Attu air is just so memorable. We also saw two bald eagles fly as high as they could and then intertwine their grasps while freefalling together; releasing their grasp at the very last second. It was so beautiful it actually made me kind of miss Jess. Anyway, the flight was good, met this guy Brad and offered him some cookies but he only took 1.

Snowy Owl

Man, I just can't seem to catch that sucker! Been up and down the Sax Zim Bog looking for it.

I won!

Well, not like it's a surprise you know. But yah, champion once again. I'm gonna have to update my vanity license plate now from 732 to 755. I think I'm gonna go see the birds of China next.
